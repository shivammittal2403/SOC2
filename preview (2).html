ISO 42001 Artificial Intelligence Management System Dashboard

AI Governance History

  • Pre-2022: Organizations relied on ISO 27001, ISO 27701, ISO 9001.
  • Nov 2022: ChatGPT triggered massive AI adoption.
  • July 2023: LLaMA and Ollama expanded open AI ecosystems.
  • Dec 2023: ISO/IEC 42001 released.
  • Jan 2024: Certification bodies like ANAB and UKAS prepared audits.
  • Mid-2024: First accredited ISO 42001 certifications began.

Business Case for ISO 42001

  • Risk management and regulatory compliance
  • Customer trust and reputation
  • Contract requirements from enterprise clients
  • Responsible AI governance
